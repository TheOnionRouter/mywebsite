Counter-Strike is a popular multiplayer first-person shooter game developed by Valve Corporation. Originally released as a modification for Half-Life, it has since become its own franchise, with several iterations and spin-offs.

Gameplay

In Counter-Strike, players join either the terrorist team or the counter-terrorist team and compete in rounds of objective-based gameplay. The objectives typically include planting or defusing bombs, rescuing hostages, or eliminating the opposing team.

Versions

Counter-Strike has undergone several major releases, including:

  • Counter-Strike (2000)
  • Counter-Strike: Condition Zero (2004)
  • Counter-Strike: Source (2004)
  • Counter-Strike: Global Offensive (2012)

Community

Counter-Strike has a large and dedicated community of players, with professional esports tournaments held regularly. It also has a vibrant modding community, creating custom maps, skins, and other content.
